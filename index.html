Technologist

Technologist is a higher-level course aimed at the job market. It is also known as a technology course or technological degree and has a shorter duration than bachelor's or degree courses: two to three years.

Bachelor degree

The bachelor's degree is a higher-level education that leads to a bachelor's degree. With an average duration of between 3 and 6 years, the bachelor's degree prepares generalist professionals with solid knowledge of the basis of a profession.

Graduation

A degree is a type of higher education course that offers its students content aimed at teaching in a specific area of ​​knowledge. Therefore, degree courses focus on training teachers to work in basic education.
